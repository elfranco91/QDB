Local Amenities

Porta10 is a little gem of a snack bar it's within a couple of minutes' walk from the apartment. The owner Armando is a gentleman, very friendly and accommodating and will adjust any dish to suit you.
Really nice and clean venue, food and drinks are very cheap and they have a particularly good variety of hot dogs. There is plenty of other dishes to choose from including breakfast. If you are looking for a good value meal I couldn't recommend it more.
Maxi Pizza next door also do great pizzas for eating in or take away if you dont fancy going out. Then you have BC's a few doors down really nice portuguese resturant with great food and great prices.
The bus stop for the old Town is directly opposite on the other side of the road, Making Porta 10 is very handy for a swift one on the way down Town.
