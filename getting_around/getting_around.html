Getting Around Albufeira Portugal

Getting Around Albufeira is easy enough there are plenty of Bus Routes and Taxi's The line Azul will take you down to the Old Town and beach in under 5 mins. where you will have lots of bars and restaurants to choose from.
The Bus stop is only a few mins walk from the apartment, busses come on time every 30 mins Mon-Friday or every hour on weekends and bank holidays.
Stay on the bus after the old town drop off and it will leave you outside the Modelo shopping centre a few more minutes on the bus will bring you into Montechoro where there is plenty of bars of restaurants, albeit the clientele is much younger here than the old townbr There are a few Taxi ranks doted around Albufeira and Montechoro, down the old town, outside Modello, Montechoro strip and you will also find taxis waiting outside Lidil and Aldi
Taxis are cheap enough, they can be anything between €4.50 and €6 to get back to the apartment from the old town depending on the time of day.
