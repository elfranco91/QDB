Albufeira one bedroom ground floor apartment

This ground floor one bedroom apartment is located near enough to the Old Town and beach area for you to walk the distance when you are in the mood for crowds, and far enough away from it all for you to enjoy peace and quiet when that’s what you feel like. The apartment has its own front door, and a courtyard with orange trees where you can sunbath, or enjoy barbecues & outdoor dining, in relative privacy. It is easy to make this conveniently located apartment a home away from home, one reason among several that persuade guests to return to it year after year. The apartment is professionally cleaned, and is well kept and maintained throughout the year. The communal pool area, which also has a children’s pool, is secure and can only be accessed by residents. There is also an adjacent studio available for rent. Each apartment can be booked separately (the one bedroom apartment sleeps four and the studio sleeps two) or together for larger groups. TV, internet and the kitchen is equiped with all the usual utilities
