Car Rental

To rent or not to rent

I have been renting cars in portugal on and off for over 12 years, I have had some good experiences and some not so good. The not so good was on the day I was returning to the airport, I noticed some damage to the side of the car, the car had been parked out on the main road and not in the parking area next to the apartment(lesson learned)

When I dropped the car back, the company said the damage would cost me €1,200, the deposit they had reserved on my credit card. Needless to say it took me a while before I rented again.

The last few years, I have been using RentalCars.com, they search for the best deal from a selection of car hire companies, last year I paid €190 for 2 weeks in june and €28 for 4 days in November, on both occassions it came with a special offer of one extra driver included, which was great as my wife also drives.

Both times the company we used through RentalCars.com was called Guerin Cars And I found them very pleasant to deal with and very quick to process on arrival and departure and the price was fantastic in my opnion. I just get the basic insurance which is included in the rental, this doesn’t include excess for damages, Theft, Vandalism, tyes and windscreen etc…

 

For all the extra cover I use CarHireExcess.ie, you can get cover just for the dates you are traveling, as I travel a few times a year to portugal I get the annual cover which works out at €48:99 I find this way a lot cheaper because, if you get the full cover of the company you rent from in Portugal, it can cost you double or even more then the origanal cost of the car hire. When you are asked upon arrival about the extra full insurance just inform them you have your own and your all set….
