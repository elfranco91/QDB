Renting a car in portugal

I have been renting cars in portugal for years now, most have went well in fairness only on one occassion the car was damaged on the street and I had to pay 12,00 in access fees..NOT NICE

For the last few years i have used a company called rentalcars.com, price wise they seem like one of the most competitive around,

when you go to book you can choose from different vendors that  operate out of Faro airport, personally i have choose Guiren the last few years as i find them to be very pleasant and quick to get all the paper work in order and dropping off the car is quick and easy.
