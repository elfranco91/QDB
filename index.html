Quinta Dos Brejos(Farm of Brejos)

Albufeira is one of the most popular Tourist destinations in Portugals Algarve
Built on an old farm, this beautiful holiday complex has great character,the original
stylish farmhouse pictured left overlooks the apartment and can be viewed from
the bedroom window.
The area surronding Quinta Dos Brejos is known as the sport area, home to the
Albufeira stadium and the skate park it has become a highly sought after Area
for buyers in recent years.

The Apartment

A large patio area overlooks a beautifully maintained garden, giving plenty of space
for the kids to run around, the patio area has table, chairs, sun loungers and also a
BBQ for dining alfresco. The pool is also surrounded by a well maintained communal garden area.

Overall the complex is very quiet and is an ideal spot to relax and watch the days drift by,the complex has a mixture of expats and Portuguese residents, giving it a very unique vibe. There is also high speed internet access with Wi-Fi Fibre optic broadband.

The Apartment


The bedroom has a double bed,the apartment sleeps up to 4 adults in total + baby in a baby cot(supplied). The living room is quite large and has a fold out double couch bed suitable for children, there is also an option of an electric blow up double bed which would be better for Adults.
The living room has table, chairs, flat scrren TV, old playstation2 and a few games. the flat screen tv takes a USB so you can bring your own media. High speed WiFi is available.The Kitchen comes fully equiped with a fridge, washing machine, microwave, kettle toaster, Iorn and all the usual utensils.

Albufeira Old Town


Albufeira Old Town Square (Jardim PÃºblico de Albufeira) is the beating heart of the atmospheric Albufeira Old Town.This tree-shaded old town square offers superb dining excellent choice of restaurants and bars, also great for people watching, shopping or just watching the day drift by.
Cobblestone streets with mosaic patterns lead in all directions from the square and then weave around the Old Town.
Visit Albufeira Old Town Square at any time of the day to witness a true essence of this ancient fishing village.

The Beach


Praia Dos Pescadores is an absolutely fantastic beach located in the Old Town. The beach is pristine with lovely clean sand,crystal clear waters and innumerable pubs,bars and restaurants close by.
This is certainly one of the most popular beaches in Albufeira, the Old Town location means it's popular with both locals and tourists alike.There are some great resturants just off the beach in the fishermans warf area. O farrals resturant and beach bar is not to be missed.
