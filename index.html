Quinta Dos Brejos(Farm of Brejos)

Quinta Dos Brejos

This large one bedroom apartment for rent in Albufeira has a large patio area overlooking beautifully maintained gardens, giving plenty of space for the kids to run around, the patio area has a table, chairs, sun loungers and a BBQ for dining alfresco.
The pool area is also surrounded by a well maintained communal garden area and also contains a toddler pool. Overall the complex is very quiet and is an ideal spot to relax and watch the days drift by,the complex has a mixture of expats and Portuguese residents, giving it a very unique vibe.

Albufeira

Albufeira is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the Algarve. Built on an old farm, this beautiful condominium has great character, the original stylish farmhouse above overlooks the apartment, which can be viewed from the bedroom window.
The area surrounding Quinta Dos Brejos is known as the sports area, home to the Albufeira stadium & the skate park, it has become a highly sought after area for buyers in recent years.
There are many local bars & restaurants within walking distance of the apartment, which is situated just off the high town, north of the clock roundabout. The apartment is ideally located in between the strip and the oldtown. Making it an ideal apartment for Families.

The Apartment


The Apartment sleeps up to 4 adults in total + a baby in a baby cot(supplied) the bedroom has a double bed. The living room is quite large and has a fold out double couch bed suitable for children, there is also an option of an electric blow up double bed which would be better for Adults.
The living room has a table, chairs, flat screen TV, an old playstation2 and a few games. the flat screen tv takes a USB so you can bring your own media. High speed WiFi is available.The Kitchen comes fully equiped with a fridge, washing machine, microwave, kettle toaster, Iorn and all the usual utensils.

Albufeira Old Town


Albufeira Old Town Square (Jardim Público de Albufeira) is the beating heart of the atmospheric Albufeira Old Town.This tree-shaded old town square offers superb dining excellent choice of restaurants and bars, also great for people watching, shopping or just watching the day drift by.
Cobblestone streets with mosaic patterns lead in all directions from the square and then weave around the Old Town.
Visit Albufeira Old Town Square at any time of the day to witness a true essence of this ancient fishing village.

The Beach


Praia Dos Pescadores is an absolutely fantastic beach located in the Old Town. The beach is pristine with lovely clean sand,crystal clear waters and innumerable pubs,bars and restaurants close by.
This is certainly one of the most popular beaches in Albufeira, the Old Town location means it's popular with both locals and tourists alike.There are some great resturants just off the beach in the fishermans warf area. O farrals resturant and beach bar is not to be missed.
