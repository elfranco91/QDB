Quinta Dos Brejos(Farm of Brejos)

Built on an old farm, this beautiful holiday complex has great character, the original stylish farmhouse pictured left overlooks the apartment and can be viewed from the bedroom window.
The area surronding Quinta Dos Brejos is known as the sport area, home to the Albufeira stadium and the skate park it has become a highly sought after Area for buyers in recent years.

The Apartment

A large patio area overlooks a beautifully maintained garden, giving plenty of space for the kids to run around, the patio area has table, chairs, sun loungers and also a BBQ for dining alfresco.
The pool is also surrounded by a well maintained communal garden area.
Overall the complex is very quiet and is an ideal spot to relax and watch the days drift by, the complex has a mixture of expats and Portuguese residents, giving it a very unique vibe.

There is also high speed internet access with Wi-Fi Fibre optic broadband.
