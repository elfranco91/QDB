Local Amenities

Porta 10 is a little gem of a snack bar it's within a couple of minutes walk from the apartment. The owner Armando is a gentleman, very friendly and accommodating and will adjust any dish to suit you.
Really nice and clean venue, food and drinks are very cheap and they have a particularly good variety of hot dogs. There is plenty of other dishes to choose from including breakfast. If you are looking for a good value meal I couldn't recommend it more.
Maxi Pizza next door also do great pizzas for eating in or take away if you dont fancy going out. Then you have BC's a few doors down really nice portuguese resturant with great food and great prices.
The bus stop for the old Town is directly opposite on the other side of the road, making Porta 10 very handy for a swift one on the way down Town.

Kiosk Alfarrobeira


This is a great place to chill and have a couple of sundowners, overlooking a kids playground with slides climbing frames climbing walls etc..Plenty to keep the kids busy. It’s only a 5 min walk from the apartment just a few hundred meters down from the bus stop They also do great toasties if you just want a snack. Overlooking Albufeira stadium, there's always a great atmosphere on match day.

Shopping


There are two main supermarkets in Albufeira Modelo and Pingo Doce. Modelo shopping center has many shops to choose from, clothes, sports, electrical and resturants to name a few, they are situated opposite each other in the High Town about a 15 min walk from the apt, alternatively you can get the bus down which will take a couple of minutes and leaves you right outside. In order to get back the taxi rank is outside Modelo ,cost is only €4/5.

Family pubs


Erins isle Irish Pub in Montechoro is about a 15/20 min walk away at the very top of the strip, it's also a great spot for kids entertainment, as is Fat Cats an English run pub about a 20 min walk from the apartment. Let's not forget Albufeira old town square in the evening, the puppet man arrives here most nights in the evening from about 9pm and will keep the kids and Adults entertained. You will be spoiled for choice for restaurants and bars overlooking the square

Marina


There are some great boat trips to be had that visit the caves, they vary in length, you can go on a 4 hour trip that includes a BBQ on a private beach or a 2 hour trip that stops at the caves for a swim , you can purchase tickets at one of the many tourist kiosk around the town. All boat trips depart from the Marina which is also a lovely spot for a drink and bite to eat.

Central Bus Station


The main Albufeira Bus station is about a 5 min walk from the apt, as you come out of the complex take a right … if you fancy going on a day trip to any of the neighbouring towns like Silves, Carvoeiro, Lagoa e there are daily buses regularly. This is also where you can pick up a trip card for €3 and add journeys to it saving you about 40% on each journey on the local busses that will bring down you the town

Great Resturants


There is an array of restaurants to cater for all tastes in The Old town, A Ruina seafood restaurant overlooking the beach is a fabulous choice, try the Fisherman’s Hut for great specials on seabass and golden bream priced at €9.95.. Urban Pizza if you feel like pizza! . Louisiana on The Fisherman’s Wharf offers a great breakfast ,there is a host of other restaurants on the Fisherman’s Wharf all with amazing sea views.

Zoo Marine


Zoomarine Located in Guia, just a few kilometers from Albufeira, you can watch presentations with dolphins, sea lions, tropical birds, birds of prey. There is also aligators and an aquarium with sharks, 4D Cinema, man made beach and wave pool and numerous other attractions and amusements, that will surely make your visit to Zoomarine even more unforgettable.

Slide and Splash


Slide & Splash is one of the best and largest water parks in Europe situated in Lagao about a 25 min drive from Albufeira ,with a total area of 7 hectares, the park offers many aquatic entertainments for all tastes and ages, as well as lawns or sun loungers, zones with animal shows, massage and fish spa, several restaurants and a shop. Price for Child: €20, Adult €27.

Parque Adventura


Parque Adventura Great fun for the kids, High Ropes Courses is a height activity leading you from tree to tree where you'll find different courses and several challenges such as bridges, rope nets and giant zip lines! .
